School Closures by Month

  • August 2026: School closed for most of August. Existing pupils return on 31 August.
  • September 2026: New pupils return on 2 September.
  • October 2026: School closed 26–30 October.
  • November 2026: School closed 30 November.
  • December 2026: School closed 23–25 December and 28–31 December.
  • January 2027: School closed 1 January and 4–5 January.
  • February 2027: School closed 1 February and 18–19 February.
  • March 2027: School closed 22–26 March and 29–31 March.
  • April 2027: School closed 1–2 April.
  • May 2027: School closed 3–4 May.
  • June 2027: School closed 3–4 June and 7–8 June. Last day of school is 30 June.
  • July 2027: School closed for the month of July.
