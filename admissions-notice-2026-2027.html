Admissions Notice

Annual Admission Notice for the 2026–2027 School Year

This notice relates to admissions to Cara Junior School for the 2026–2027 school year.

Cara Junior School caters for pupils aged 4–12 years old with a diagnosis of Autism Spectrum Disorder and a mild or moderate intellectual disability.

A copy of the school’s Admission Policy and Application Form for Admission is available to download from the school website or by request from the school office.

Admission Policy and Application Form

A copy of the school’s Admission Policy and the Application Form for Admission for 2026–2027 is available as follows:

  • To download at: www.carajuniorschool.ie
  • On request by emailing: office@carajs.ie

Application and Decision Dates

The school will commence accepting applications on 1 October 2025
The school shall cease accepting applications on 15 January 2026
Applicants will be notified of the decision by 31 January 2026
Applicants must confirm acceptance of an offer by 15 February 2026
Failure to accept an offer within the prescribed period may result in the offer being withdrawn. Late applications will be considered and decided on in accordance with the school’s Admission Policy.
