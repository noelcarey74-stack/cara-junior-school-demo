Terms of Use

These terms explain how visitors may use the Cara Junior School website.

School Website

Terms of Use

Using this website

This website is operated for Cara Junior School to provide general school information, notices, policies, calendar details, class information and contact details.

General information

The information on this website is provided for general school communication. While every effort is made to keep information accurate and up to date, details may change from time to time.

Parents and guardians should contact the school office if they need confirmation of any important information.

School policies and documents

Policy documents and PDF files may be reviewed or updated by the school. Visitors should always refer to the latest version published on the website or provided by the school office.

Photos and school content

Photographs, text, school updates and website content are provided for school communication and should not be copied, reused or republished without permission.

Cara Junior School takes pupil privacy seriously. Approved photographs and class updates should be used respectfully.

External links

This website may include links to external websites or third-party services. Cara Junior School is not responsible for the content, availability or privacy practices of external websites.

Website availability

The school aims to keep the website available and useful, but access may be interrupted from time to time due to maintenance, technical issues or updates.

Misuse of the website

Visitors must not misuse the website, attempt to interfere with its operation, or use any information from the website in a harmful, misleading or inappropriate way.
