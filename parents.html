Home and School

Working together

Strong communication between home and school is important. Parents and guardians are encouraged to contact the school office if they need information, support or guidance.

Cara Junior School values respectful communication and partnership with families. The school office can direct queries to the most appropriate person.

  • Contact the office for general school questions.
  • Meetings can be arranged through the school office.
  • For urgent or important matters, please phone the school directly.

Privacy and Safety

School communications

The school website should only publish approved information, photographs and documents.

  • Class updates and photographs should be checked and approved by the school.
  • Pupil names should not be published with photographs unless approved by the school.
  • Official documents should be kept up to date in the school policy library.
