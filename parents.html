Home and School

Working together

Strong communication between home and school is important. Parents and guardians are encouraged to contact the school office if they need information, support or guidance.

Cara Junior School values respectful communication and partnership with families. The school office can direct queries to the most appropriate person.

For urgent or important matters, parents and guardians should contact the school directly by phone.

Privacy and Safety

School communications

The school website should only publish approved information, photographs and documents.

For pupil privacy, photographs and class updates should be checked and approved by the school before they are added to the website.

Children’s names should not be published with photographs unless this has been specifically approved by the school.
