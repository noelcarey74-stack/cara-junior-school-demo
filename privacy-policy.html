Privacy Policy

Cara Junior School website privacy information

Cara Junior School operates this website to provide information for parents, guardians, staff, visitors and the wider school community.

This website is intended to provide general school information, including school news, policies, admissions information, calendar details and contact information.

Information collection

This website does not knowingly collect personal information from visitors through a contact form or online account system.

If a visitor contacts the school by phone or email, the information provided will be handled by the school in line with normal school procedures and applicable data protection requirements.

Website hosting

The website may be hosted by a third-party hosting provider. Hosting providers may process limited technical information required to deliver and secure the website.

Cookies

This website does not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies. Please see the Cookie Policy page for more information.

External links

This website may contain links to external websites or documents. Cara Junior School is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external websites.

Children’s privacy

Cara Junior School takes pupil privacy seriously. Photographs and school updates should only be published where they have been approved by the school.

Children’s names should not be published with photographs unless this has been specifically approved by the school.

Updates to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be published on this page.
