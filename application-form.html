Before applying

  • Please read the Admissions and Enrolment Policy.
  • Please check the Admissions Notice for key dates and available places.
  • Please complete the official PDF form clearly.
  • Please contact the school office if you are unsure about any part of the process.
Completing an application form and placing a child’s name on a list does not automatically give the child a right to a place in the school. Parents and guardians should refer to the official admissions documents for full details.
