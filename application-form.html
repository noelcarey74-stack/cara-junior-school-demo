Important Notes

  • Completion of an application form and placement of the child’s name on a list does not automatically give the right to a place in the school.
  • A family may not be successful in obtaining a place in the year requested and may have to reapply the following year.
  • There is no onus on the Department of Education and Skills to arrange transport or pay towards private transport arrangements to any school other than the nearest recognised mainstream school, special class or school.
