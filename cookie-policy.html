Cookie Policy

How this website uses cookies

Cookies are small text files that may be stored on a visitor’s device when they use a website.

Advertising and tracking cookies

This website does not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies.

Essential cookies

Essential cookies may be used only where needed for the website to work properly or to remember a visitor’s cookie preference.

Third-party services

If the website includes external services, such as embedded maps, videos or third-party links, those services may use their own cookies or privacy settings.

Visitors should review the privacy and cookie information provided by those third-party services.

Managing cookies

Visitors can manage or delete cookies through their web browser settings. The steps will depend on the browser and device being used.

Updates to this policy

This cookie policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be published on this page.
